Outcome Rating Scale (ORS)

The ORS is a brief, 4-item measure designed to assess areas of life functioning known to change as a result of therapeutic intervention. It takes less than one minute to complete and score.

  • • Individual well-being
  • • Interpersonal relationships
  • • Social functioning
  • • Overall well-being

Session Rating Scale (SRS)

The SRS is a brief, 4-item measure designed to assess the therapeutic alliance. It provides immediate feedback on the quality of the relationship and allows for course correction in real time.

  • • Relationship quality
  • • Goals and topics
  • • Approach or method
  • • Overall session quality

Usage Guidelines

Purchasing a license provides users with:

  • ▸ A lifetime license (subject to the terms and conditions of the licensing agreement) to use the ORS and SRS in paper and pencil format
  • ▸ A file containing the official SRS and ORS forms that may be downloaded, distributed to and printed by users

NO ELECTRONIC OR DIGITAL USE OF THE SCALES IS PERMITTED.

Several authorized vendors provide systems for administering, scoring, and interpreting the scales. For more information on electronic versions, please visit the FIT Software Tools section.
